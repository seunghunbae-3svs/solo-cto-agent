# Purpose
Draft short, credible pitch content without inflated claims.

# Use When
- You need a one-pager or short deck draft
- You want to clarify problem/solution/traction
- You want messaging that is founder-credible

# Output Contract
- A short pitch outline (6-10 bullets)
- 1-2 alternative headlines
- 3 proof points or placeholders for evidence

# Guardrails
- Do not invent traction or metrics
- Use placeholders when data is missing
- Keep tone direct and grounded
